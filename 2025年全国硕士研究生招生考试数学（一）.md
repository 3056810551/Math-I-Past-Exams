18. (本题满分 12 分)

$$
x^2 \frac{\partial^2 g}{\partial x^2} + xy \frac{\partial^2 g}{\partial x\partial y} + y^2 \frac{\partial^2 g}{\partial y^2} = 1,
$$

且 $g(x,x) = 1$，$\left. \frac{\partial g}{\partial x} \right|_{(x,x)} = \frac{2}{x}$，求 $f(u)$。

22. (本题满分 12 分)

$$
Y =
\begin{cases}
0, & X \le 100, \\
X - 100, & X > 100.
\end{cases}
$$

$$
f(x) =

0, & x \le 0.
\end{cases}
$$
